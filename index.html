Controls

  • WASD or ↑ ↓ ← → — Move
  • Enter — Inspect / Interact
  • Esc — Close popups

Tips

  • Get close to objects to interact with them (an arrow will appear)
  • Mouse clicking is also fair game
  • Try stepping through the border — you might discover a new room!
